Michael

Michael is a curious fellow. He will always greet you at the door when you come home. He is a snowshoe siamese, and he looks like all four of his paws were dipped in white paint. He also has a handsome white streak that runs from his chin to his tail.

Hershey

As a Burmese, Hershey is a cat defined by loyalty. When he finds a friend, he becomes a friend for life. He likes to sleep underneath the covers, preferably by your side where he can nuzzle and keep warm. He likes tuna, and he prefers it served in small chunks over smooth pate.

Moe

Moe is a loving black lab that has a very calm temperment and loves to cuddle. He is 1 year old and gets along well with all other animals. This includes chickens, cats, and other dogs. He enjoys long walks on the beach and bacon grease. He also loves sailing.

Bella

Bella is an 80-pound ball of love. She is extremely loyal and does not like when anyone or anything comes near the car. She is a built-in car alarm of sorts. She enjoys playing ball and pulling on the leash the entire time you are on a walk.

Flea

Flea is a calico cat with a funky tail that loves playing with chickens and other dogs. She is box trained but prefers to be outside and enjoying the weather. She weighs around 5-lbs and would love to come home with you to cuddle. Flea is very talkative and will retrieve bottle caps from certain beers. She is a great animal and deserves a great and loving home.
